Joel "The Process" Embiid

Joel Embiid is an NBA player currently playing for the Philadelphia 76ers. He is from the country of Cameroon, where he played soccer and volleyball. He was invited to a basketball camp nearby hosted by the NBA player Luc Mbah a Moute, and was eventually recruited and encouraged to come to the United States and ended up playing basketball for Kansas University. He was drafted third overall by the Philadelphia 76ers in 2014, and missed his first two seasons due to an injury. He is currently a 4x All-Star and one of the greatest big men to play the game.
